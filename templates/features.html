how much do you know about wood?

A world without wood products would have far-reaching impacts on construction, the economy, and the environment. The use of wood in building materials, furniture, paper, energy generation, and many other areas makes it an essential material. The removal of wood would increase the need for alternative materials, causing higher construction costs and intensifying environmental effects. It would also affect the forestry and wood industry, which are significant economic sectors. The importance of sustainable forestry emphasizes the need to utilize the positive properties of wood efficiently and in an environmentally friendly manner.
