The Truth about Wood

Wood is one of the most versatile and sustainable materials on Earth. It has been used for centuries to build homes, paper, packaging, and create works of art. Wood also stores Carbon and can substitute less environmental friendly ressources such as cement in construction.

In Austria, approximately 15.2 million tons of wood are used annually, which amounts to about 1.7 tons per person per year. Despite its widespread use, many people are unaware of the innovative wood products that are available today. As experts in the forestry and wood sector, we are passionate about sharing our knowledge of wood utilization and its diverse applications. This website curates some of the most interesting and innovative ways that wood is being used today.
